What is The Pydra Project?
The Pydra Project is a small organization comprised of 3 teenagers who are trying to change
the IT landscape by providing free compute power to students and non-profits with "microservers".
What's a microserver?
A microserver is a small, power efficient computer that can be remotely accessed used for learning or aiding in shared goals.
