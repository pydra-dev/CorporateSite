About

The Pydra Project consists of team of teenagers trying to change the IT industry by providing compute power to students and other individuals around the world.
Our Vision

The Pydra Project aims to provide compute power in the form of low-powered microservers such as Raspberry Pi's to assist students and other nonprofits with tools in the IT space.
